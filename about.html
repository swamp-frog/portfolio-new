About

David Mihai is a graphic design student at the Artez University, Arnhem, NL. He spends his time cutting, pasting, mashing and breaking stuff in search of visual possibilities. His works include books, zines, posters, web pages. David often works with photography and printing, going backwards and forwards between machines that capture and ones that replicate.
